# Volunteer Email Generator

## Purpose

This AI workflow helps nonprofit organizations quickly generate professional welcome emails for new volunteers.

Instead of writing each email manually, staff members only need to provide a few details.

---

## Required Information

- Volunteer Name
- Event Name
- Event Date
- Event Time
- Event Location
- Contact Person

---

## Claude Prompt

You are an AI assistant helping a nonprofit organization.

Write a professional and friendly welcome email for a new volunteer.

Include:

- Thank the volunteer
- Event name
- Date and time
- Location
- Dress code (comfortable clothes and closed-toe shoes)
- Parking information
- Contact information
- Encourage the volunteer and thank them again.

Use a warm and professional tone.

---

## Example Input

Volunteer Name: Sarah

Event: Weekend Food Distribution

Date: July 20

Time: 9:00 AM

Location: Community Center

Contact: Emily (555-123-4567)

---

## Example Output

Subject: Welcome to Our Volunteer Team!

Hello Sarah,

Thank you for volunteering for our Weekend Food Distribution event.

We are excited to have you join our team on July 20 at 9:00 AM.

Please arrive 15 minutes early. Wear comfortable clothes and closed-toe shoes. Parking is available next to the Community Center.

If you have any questions, please contact Emily at (555) 123-4567.

Thank you again for supporting our community. We look forward to seeing you!

Best regards,

Volunteer Coordinator
