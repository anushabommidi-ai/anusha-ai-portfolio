# Demo Prompts

## Prompt 1 – Volunteer Welcome Email

You are an AI assistant helping a nonprofit organization.

Write a warm welcome email for a new volunteer joining a weekend food distribution event.

Include:

- Appreciation
- Event time
- Dress code
- Parking instructions
- Contact information

---

## Prompt 2 – Meeting Summary

Summarize the following meeting notes into:

- Key decisions
- Action items
- Owners
- Deadlines

---

## Prompt 3 – FAQ Generator

Create answers for frequently asked volunteer questions such as:

- Where do I park?
- What should I wear?
- Who do I contact if I'm late?

---

## Prompt 4 – SOP Generator

Create a Standard Operating Procedure for onboarding new volunteers.

Include:

- Before arrival
- Check-in
- Safety briefing
- Assigned responsibilities
- End-of-day checkout
