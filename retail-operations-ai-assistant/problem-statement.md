# Problem Statement

Retail stores handle thousands of customer interactions every day.

Front-end managers and cashiers frequently answer repetitive operational questions such as:

- Should another register be opened?
- Which cashier should move to self-checkout?
- How should shift updates be communicated?
- How should customer concerns be documented?
- What information should be shared with the next shift?

These repetitive communication tasks consume time and often lead to inconsistent responses.

This project explores how Claude AI can support retail teams by providing standardized communication, workflow guidance, and operational assistance.
